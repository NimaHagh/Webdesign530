Welcome to Gravity Falls!

Gravity Falls: A Mystical Journey into Adventure and Mystery

Gravity Falls is a captivating American animated series created by Alex Hirsch, blending mystery, comedy, and adventure in a way that has enchanted viewers of all ages. Airing on Disney Channel and Disney XD, the show follows the summer escapades of Dipper Pines (voiced by Jason Ritter) and his quirky twin sister Mabel (voiced by Kristen Schaal). The duo is sent to the mysterious town of Gravity Falls, Oregon, to stay with their eccentric Great-Uncle, "Grunkle" Stan, who runs the oddball tourist trap known as the Mystery Shack. But the town is far from ordinary—it's teeming with supernatural creatures, hidden secrets, and bizarre happenings that lure Dipper and Mabel into thrilling adventures.

Premiering on June 15, 2012, Gravity Falls quickly became a phenomenon, running until its epic conclusion on February 15, 2016. Creator Alex Hirsch made it clear that the show wasn’t canceled but deliberately wrapped up to fulfill its carefully crafted storyline. The grand finale, "Weirdmageddon 3: Take Back the Falls," left fans in awe, while leaving the door slightly open for possible future episodes or specials. The legacy continued with the release of Journal 3, a replica of Dipper’s guidebook, the graphic novel Gravity Falls: Lost Legends in 2018, and an upcoming adult novel The Book of Bill, set for release in 2024.

The series earned widespread acclaim for its brilliant writing, engaging characters, top-notch voice acting, and stunning animation. Its humor and layered storytelling appeal to both younger audiences and adults alike, making it a multigenerational favorite. Over its run, Gravity Falls won two Emmy Awards, three Annie Awards, and a BAFTA Children’s Award, alongside several other accolades. In 2015 and early 2016, it was Disney XD's highest-rated show, breaking records and garnering a dedicated fandom that continues to thrive.

Beyond the screen, Gravity Falls has left its mark on pop culture, influencing a wave of animated shows that followed. Its fandom is passionate, creative, and ever-growing, with a wide range of official merchandise keeping the magic alive.

Mysteries of Gravity Falls

Gravity Falls is more than just a small town in Oregon—it's a hub of strange occurrences, cryptic symbols, and unsolved mysteries that keep Dipper, Mabel, and the rest of the gang on their toes. Here are some of the weirdest and wildest mysteries that have baffled the Pines twins and fans alike!



  • The Journals: Three mysterious journals written by an unknown author are the key to unlocking the supernatural secrets of Gravity Falls. Dipper finds Journal 3, packed with cryptic notes, bizarre creatures, and clues about the town's mysteries. But who wrote them, and where are the other two?


  • Bill Cipher: A dream demon who transcends reality itself. Bill appears in cryptic dreams and has his own hidden agenda. What does he want with Gravity Falls, and why does he seem to know more than anyone else?


  • The Mystery Shack’s Hidden Secrets: Grunkle Stan’s Mystery Shack is more than just a tourist trap. From secret rooms to strange artifacts, the Shack holds more than its fair share of hidden surprises. What is Stan really up to, and why is there a secret door behind the vending machine?


  • The Author of the Journals: One of the biggest questions throughout the series is: Who wrote the mysterious journals that Dipper discovers? The search for the author leads to mind-blowing revelations about the history of Gravity Falls and the true nature of its mysteries.


  • Weirdmageddon: The apocalyptic event that Bill Cipher unleashes upon Gravity Falls. Reality itself breaks down, unleashing chaos and bizarre creatures. Can Dipper, Mabel, and their friends stop Bill before it's too late?


  • Cryptograms and Hidden Messages: Throughout the show, viewers are treated to cryptic messages, ciphers, and codes hidden in the end credits and episodes. These cryptograms often hint at future plot points or reveal more about the town’s dark past. Can you crack them all?


These are just a few of the many mysteries that make *Gravity Falls* such a puzzle-filled adventure! From hidden clues in every episode to shocking revelations, the mysteries of Gravity Falls keep fans guessing at every turn. Are you ready to uncover the truth?
